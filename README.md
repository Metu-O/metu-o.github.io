## About Me

I am a Data Scientist working at the intersection of machine learning, bioinformatics, and healthcare, building models and pipelines for multimodal clinical data including medical imaging and next-generation sequencing (NGS).

My focus is on developing end-to-end ML systems that turn complex biological and clinical datasets into reliable, deployable tools that can support precision medicine and improve patient outcomes.

I have experience in hospital and biomedical research environments, contributing to projects involving genomic analysis, microbial classification, and deep learning for biomedical imaging. A key interest of mine is moving ML models beyond experimentation into production-ready clinical workflows.

I work primarily with Python-based ML stacks such as TensorFlow and PyTorch, and I build supporting data and deployment infrastructure using tools like SQL, Docker, and Django. I am also actively expanding my expertise in MLOps, cloud platforms, and scalable AI systems.

My goal is to build robust healthcare AI systems that integrate multimodal data to improve clinical decision-making, particularly in diagnosis, risk stratification, and treatment optimization.
